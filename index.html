"Your Dream Is My Story"

About Me

I feel lucky to have been born in 1997, right at the start of a new generation—Gen Z. We’re a generation that not only inherits traditional values but also gets to embrace amazing advancements in building materials and technology. This mix of the old and the new sets a strong foundation for creative and groundbreaking innovations in the future.

Growing up in this dynamic time, we’ve seen rapid changes in the way we approach design, construction, and architecture. From sustainable materials to smart home technology, the possibilities seem endless. It’s exciting to be part of a generation that can both appreciate the craftsmanship of the past and push the boundaries of what’s possible today. Every challenge feels like an opportunity to think differently, to innovate, and to create spaces that not only meet the needs of today but also anticipate the future.

ABOUT EXPERIENCE

Since 2018, I’ve had the opportunity to intern at DATAA Architecture Company and participate in significant projects such as the Doi Hong Hotel, Bambini Montessori International Kindergarten, and the Administrative Complex at Sonadezi Industrial Park. These experiences not only enhanced my professional skills but also gave me a deeper understanding of the practical aspects of the industry.

2021: After working on several architectural projects at DATAA, I began to shift my focus to a new field—interior design. At 'BEYOUR' I gained valuable experience in space design and furniture products. This helped me develop a more comprehensive vision of the construction and design industry.

In 2023, I decided to challenge myself by starting a freelance career, focusing on residential projects and service buildings. It’s a challenging step, but also an opportunity for me to freely express my creativity and bring my unique design ideas to life.

2024: I started with my own creative space, which could be called a showroom, design office, or product display studio. This will be a place where I showcase my passion through furniture, materials, and artwork. At the same time, I also dedicate time to sharing knowledge and passion with young people who wish to pursue a career in design.
